• The bulleted list is called a Unordered list. It opens with a <UL> tag and closes with <\UL> tag. It looks just like ordered list, except that the bullets appear at each <LI> tag instead of numbers.

  • The numbered list is called an Ordered list. It begins with <OL> tag and closes with <\OL> tag. Numbers and line breaks appear at each <LI> tag and entire list is indented.

  • The list in terms and their meanings is called a Defination List. It starts with <DL> and ends with <\DL> tag. The <DT> goes in front of each term to be defined, with a <DD> tag in front of each defination. Line breaks and indentation appears automatically.
